ABOUT ME

Hello I'm Jeanne!

I am a self-taught Digital Artist based in the Philippines, I am currently a college student at De La Salle Lipa pursuing a degree in Computer Science.

I find joy in crafting visuals that connect with people, whether subtly or deeply. And with technology as part of my toolset, I can push those ideas even further, bringing concepts to life through animation, interactivity, or code, and turning imagination into experiences you can see, feel, and even explore.
